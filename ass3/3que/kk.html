Indian Climate:

Summer

During the three months from April to June it is summer in most parts of India.Generally in the month of April,many parts of India experiences high temperatures.The normal temperature recorded in this time in generally 30 degrees C.But at some places in the plateau region the temperature rises to 40 degrees C and in the Himalayan mountain region, it remains in the Himalayan Mountain region,it remains near about 20 degrees C.

Winter

During the period from December to Febuary there is winter season in India During this time clouds and Dry North East Monsoons passes over different regions of India and temperature is decreased considerably. In Himalayan area the temperature is much lower(between 5-10 degrees C) But sometimes cyclone(coming from west) causes storm and rainfall which is known as Western disturbance.

Spring

The advent of spring brings us the pleasant sound wind begins to blow. It refresh our body and mind. The countryside appears to be adorned with green leaves and flowers. Birds sing and bees hum the flower garden. Trees and creepers are covered with beautiful flowers, Bees hum all day long among them.

Rainy

The rainy season in India begins generally from the second week of July and continues up to the second week of October.From the second week of June Humid monsoon enters various parts of India and causes rainfall everywhere in the state.
